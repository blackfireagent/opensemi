Automated microscope tracks directional heat flow in thermoelectric materials for green energy
A new thermal diffusivity microscope developed by researchers at DTU and other institutions allows for high-resolution measurements of heat flow in materials vital for thermoelectric applications. This innovation could significantly impact the design and efficiency of future energy systems and electronic devices.

As semiconductor features shrink below 5nm, e-beam inspection technology becomes critical for detecting defects that traditional methods miss.
