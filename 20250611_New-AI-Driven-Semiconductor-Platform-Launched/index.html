Determinants Of Bond Wave Speed In Wafer Bonding
A recent study conducted by researchers from Yokohama National University and Tokyo Electron Kyushu Limited explores the critical factors influencing bond wave speed in wafer bonding. This research is pivotal for advancing 3D architectures used in logic, memory, and CMOS image sensors, emphasizing the importance of minimizing wafer distortion for precise subsequent processes. The study highlights that while plasma activation enhances bond wave speed through increased Si–OH groups on the surface, excessive high-power plasma can negatively affect speed due to adsorbed water.
